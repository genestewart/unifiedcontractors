# Product Mission

> Last Updated: 2025-07-31
> Version: 1.0.0

## Pitch

Unified Contractors is a professional multi-service construction website that showcases the company's expertise in custom home building, design services, remodeling, water mitigation, and sump pump systems by providing a comprehensive portfolio and lead generation platform.

## Users

### Primary Customers

- **Property Owners in Park City Area**: Homeowners and business owners seeking construction, remodeling, or water mitigation services
- **Real Estate Developers**: Professionals needing custom home building and design services for new developments
- **Property Managers**: Those responsible for maintaining commercial and residential properties requiring water damage solutions

### User Personas

**Affluent Homeowner** (35-65 years old)
- **Role:** Property owner in Park City or surrounding areas
- **Context:** Owns luxury mountain home requiring specialized construction services
- **Pain Points:** Finding reliable contractors who understand mountain construction, water damage from snow melt
- **Goals:** Protect investment, enhance property value, ensure quality craftsmanship

**Commercial Property Manager** (30-55 years old)
- **Role:** Manages multiple commercial properties
- **Context:** Responsible for maintaining building integrity and tenant satisfaction
- **Pain Points:** Emergency water damage response, finding contractors for multiple services
- **Goals:** Minimize downtime, prevent costly water damage, maintain property standards

## The Problem

### Fragmented Service Providers

Most construction companies in the Park City area specialize in only 2-3 services, forcing property owners to hire multiple contractors for comprehensive projects. This leads to coordination challenges and inconsistent quality.

**Our Solution:** Offer all five core services under one roof with unified project management.

### Lack of Water Damage Integration

Traditional construction companies treat water mitigation as separate from building services. Property owners face delays when water issues arise during construction or remodeling projects.

**Our Solution:** Integrated water mitigation and sump pump expertise within our construction services.

### Trust and Credibility Concerns

Property owners struggle to evaluate contractor expertise and reliability before committing to expensive projects. Many contractors lack comprehensive online presence showcasing their capabilities.

**Our Solution:** Professional website with detailed service information, portfolio, and clear credibility markers.

## Differentiators

### Comprehensive Service Integration

Unlike competitors who offer 2-3 services, we provide all five core services: custom home building, design, remodeling, water mitigation, and sump pump systems. This results in seamless project execution and single-point accountability.

### Emergency Response Capability

Unlike traditional construction companies, we maintain 24/7 emergency water damage response capabilities. This results in faster problem resolution and prevention of secondary damage during active projects.

### Mountain Construction Expertise

Unlike general contractors, we specialize in Park City's unique high-altitude building requirements and seasonal challenges. This results in better-performing structures designed for extreme weather conditions.

## Key Features

### Core Features

- **Service Showcase Pages:** Dedicated pages for each of the five core services with detailed descriptions and benefits
- **Portfolio Gallery:** Visual showcase of completed projects organized by service category
- **Lead Generation Forms:** Strategic contact forms on each page optimized for conversion
- **Emergency Contact System:** Prominent 24/7 emergency response information for water damage services

### Collaboration Features

- **Project Inquiry System:** Detailed forms capturing project specifics for accurate initial consultations
- **Service Area Map:** Interactive display of coverage areas across Park City and surrounding counties
- **Resource Center:** Educational content about construction, water damage prevention, and maintenance

### Trust-Building Features

- **Credentials Display:** Prominent licensing, certifications, and years of experience
- **Client Testimonials:** Organized reviews by service type with project details
- **Process Transparency:** Clear explanation of project workflows for each service
